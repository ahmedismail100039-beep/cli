# Baby Bibo's Lost Teddy — Kids Video Storyboard

A kid-friendly story with soft, gentle animation for babies and toddlers. Teddy goes missing, Bibo searches, happy hug at the end.

Feed these shots one at a time into your video tool (Veo, Sora, Pika, etc.), then join them in CapCut.

## Shot 1 — Happy morning

Soft, glossy 3D animation in a gentle nursery-rhyme cartoon style for babies and toddlers. A cute, friendly toddler named Bibo with a big round head, huge happy eyes, chubby rosy cheeks, and a cozy yellow onesie giggles softly while playing with his fluffy brown teddy bear on a sunny living-room rug. Warm morning light, pastel colors, rounded safe shapes, slow gentle bouncy motion, calm cheerful mood. Vertical 9:16. No text.

## Shot 2 — Where's teddy?

Same gentle 3D cartoon style and toddler Bibo. He looks around the cozy room and his big eyes get curious and a little surprised — teddy is gone. He gives a sweet, innocent "oh?" gesture with his little hands. Soft lighting, friendly non-scary expression, pastel palette, calm mood. Vertical 9:16. No text.

## Shot 3 — Gentle search

Same toddler Bibo happily tiptoeing through a bright, friendly cartoon house, peeking under a soft blue cushion and behind a smiling potted plant. Cute curious expression, playful slow tip-toe steps, glossy 3D look, soft cheerful colors, safe rounded furniture. Vertical 9:16, gentle motion. No text.

## Shot 4 — Found you!

Same toddler Bibo in a sunny, friendly cartoon backyard, spotting his fluffy teddy bear sitting on a soft red swing. His face lights up with sweet, gentle joy, little arms raised happily. Green lawn, fluffy white clouds, warm soft sunlight, glossy wholesome 3D nursery-rhyme style. Vertical 9:16, calm happy motion. No text.

## Shot 5 — Big happy hug

Same toddler Bibo gently scooping up his teddy bear for a warm, loving hug, with a soft happy giggle and a slow twirl. Twinkly friendly sparkles, golden warm light, soft pastel colors, cozy heartwarming mood. Glossy gentle 3D cartoon style, vertical 9:16, smooth slow motion. No text.

## What makes these toddler-safe:

- Slow, gentle motion instead of fast or jumpy — easier on little eyes.
- No scary or sad beats — teddy is just "missing," never lost in a worrying way, and the worry is mild and quickly resolved.
- Soft rounded shapes and warm light — the calm, cozy look real baby channels use.
- Consistent friendly Bibo in every shot so kids recognize him.

# "Where's My Teddy?" — Sing-Along Song

## Verse 1 — happy morning

Good morning, Bibo, time to play,
With teddy bear we'll play all day!
Soft and fluffy, brown and sweet,
Teddy bear is such a treat!

## Chorus

Where's my teddy? Where'd he go?
Up high or down low?
Where's my teddy? Let's go see!
Come and find him, one-two-three!

## Verse 2 — the search

Tiptoe, tiptoe, look around,
Is teddy bear here to be found?
Under the cushion, behind the chair,
Where, oh where, is teddy bear?

## Chorus

Where's my teddy? Where'd he go?
Up high or down low?
Where's my teddy? Let's go see!
Come and find him, one-two-three!

## Verse 3 — found him!

On the swing, hooray hooray!
Teddy bear was there to play!
Big warm hug and round we spin,
Bibo's smiling ear to ear-grin!

## Final Chorus — soft and slow

Found my teddy, yes we did,
Happy teddy, happy kid!
Found my teddy, hug him tight,
Now it's time to say goodnight!

## Suno Prompt for Music

Gentle nursery-rhyme children's song, sweet female vocalist singing softly and clearly, simple cheerful melody, light twinkly xylophone, soft ukulele and gentle piano, slow happy tempo, warm wholesome toddler-friendly mood, clean and bright. Loopable, calm and joyful.

### Tips:

- In Suno, paste the lyrics into the lyrics box and the description above into the style box.
- Ask for it slow and gentle — if the first version feels too fast for little ones, regenerate with "slower tempo, lullaby-like."
- Match each verse to its shots in CapCut: Verse 1 → Shot 1, Chorus → Shot 2, Verse 2 → Shot 3, and so on. The song and visuals will line up naturally.
